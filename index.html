08 · Known Limitations

Where our system falls short

Simulated GPS Traces

All 420 GPS trajectories were generated via correlated random walk simulation seeded from OSM anchor points — not collected from real devices. Density scores may not reflect true visitor movement.

Limited Sentiment Coverage

YouTube sentiment was collected for only 8 of 24 POIs. Remaining 16 locations lack review signals, reducing retrieval accuracy for queries about less-popular sites.

English-Only Reviews

The cardiffnlp XLM-RoBERTa model was applied to English-filtered comments only. Thai-language and Chinese-language visitor opinions are excluded, skewing sentiment toward Western tourists.

Static Knowledge Snapshot

The FAISS index and sentiment data are not updated in real time. New events, seasonal spikes, or viral trends will not be reflected until the pipeline is re-run manually.

Single LLM Benchmark

Evaluation covers LLaMA 3.1 8B via Groq only. No ablation against larger models — whether GPT-4o or Claude 3 would improve Hit Rate further is untested.

09 · Future Discussion

Expanding to every type of tourism

How agentic RAG could adapt to different visitor profiles and seasonal rhythms

Tourism Types

Cultural & Heritage Tourism

Personalise temple route recommendations based on real-time crowd density. Pair GNSS data with UNESCO heritage guidelines to flag conservation-risk thresholds at Wat Chedi Luang and Doi Suthep.

Eco & Adventure Tourism

Integrate trail occupancy sensors and weather APIs for Doi Inthanon, Mae Rim, and Mae Taeng. Predict carrying-capacity exceedance and reroute trekkers via low-density corridors.

Gastronomic Tourism

Mine Thai-language Wongnai, Google Maps, and TikTok reviews alongside YouTube. Build a separate "food-focused" vector store to answer queries like "least-crowded authentic khao soi spots".

Wellness & Meditation Tourism

Track retreat occupancy at Wat Suan Dok monk-chat programs and Doi Pui wellness centres. Surface sentiment from long-stay visitors rather than day-trippers to capture deeper experience quality.

Community-Based Tourism

Partner with Chiang Mai CBT networks to ingest visitor booking data and local household income signals — enabling the agent to recommend high-impact, low-density village experiences.

Seasonal Dynamics

Cool Season · Nov – Feb

Peak tourist influx — overflow risk at top-8 POIs is highest. The system should issue proactive congestion alerts and suggest time-slot booking for Doi Suthep and Sunday Walking Street.

Hot Season · Mar – May

Smoke-haze from agricultural burning (PM2.5 spikes) dramatically affects outdoor experience. Future agent tools should query AQI APIs and recommend indoor alternatives like MAYA or Central Festival.

Rainy / Green Season · Jun – Oct

Visitor numbers drop — an opportunity to promote off-peak cultural immersion. RAG retrieval should surface local festival events (Asanha Bucha, Ok Phansa) to attract niche travellers.

Yi Peng & Loy Krathong · Nov

Extraordinary density spike around the old city moat. The agent could deploy a short-term crowd-surge model trained on historical GPS surges to guide safe viewing positions and exit routes.

Real-Time Seasonal Index

Combine TAT tourism statistics, Google Trends queries ("Chiang Mai travel"), and flight search volume into a seasonal demand index — letting the agent self-calibrate density thresholds year-round.
